The Scenes Of The Play

  1. Act I. The Octagon Room in Sir Robert Chiltern’s House in Grosvenor Square.
  2. Act II. Morning-room in Sir Robert Chiltern’s House.
  3. Act III. The Library of Lord Goring’s House in Curzon Street.
  4. Act IV. Same as Act II.

Time: The Present

Place: London.

The action of the play is completed within twenty-four hours.

Location: THEATRE ROYAL, HAYMARKET
